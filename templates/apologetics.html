Does Truth Exist?

As Frank Turek puts it: if no people lived on the earth, would it be true
that no people lived on the earth?
Yes!
Therefore, truth exists apart from mankind.

We expect truth in our paychecks.
We expect truth at the bank.
We expect truth everywhere. Don't we?

Does God Exist?

While we can't completely prove that there is a God,
it can't be disproved either.
As R.C. Sproul put it, to prove that God does not exist would require a person
to be able to examine the entire universe in every possible dimension
at the same time.
And just in case he missed something, do it again, and again.

When we see the irreducible complexity (per Michael Behe)
it is irrational to deny that there is a designer.
Even if someone produces life in the lab, they prove intelligent design.
Why? Because it required intelligence to produce that life.

No cause can regress into infinity. Because we are at today
we know there was a yesterday, and a day before. But it can't go
back forever. Because we have time, everything 'started' at some point.
This required a being who could create time, space and matter, and yet be
outside time, space and matter. This being had to be very powerful.
This being must have a mind to 'decide' to create.
This being sounds an awful lot like what the Bible calls God.

Did Jesus Rise from the Dead?

Nearly all biblical scholars, even the atheists, accept that Jesus was a real person.
Ancient texts also claim that Jesus was a wonder worker.
We also have many ancient texts, along with archeological evidence to prove that
Pontius Pilate was a real person and Jesus was crucified and died outside Jerusalem.
We also have ancient texts that mention that Jesus rose from the dead, was seen alive
made appearances to people, and walked, talked and ate for about 40 days.

If anyone can prove that Jesus did not rise from the dead, Christianity would be over.
But we are still here!
