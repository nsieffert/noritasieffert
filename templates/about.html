Dr. Sieffert, Pastor, Author, Speaker, Musician

Wife, Mother, Grandmother, Farmer

computer geek, coder, crocheter



Dr. Sieffert is the Senior Pastor at Faith Fellowship Church of the Nazarene in Texas. Born number six in a family of nine, Dr. Sieffert grew up in Illinois near the Wisconsin border. She married her high school sweetheart, John Sieffert, in 1982 and together they have two children and six grandchildren. These days Dr. Sieffert writes and speaks publically, along with dabbling in website building and Python coding. She has a hobby farm with her husband and they raise a cow, a goat, a couple of ponies, and a lot of chickens.


Watching her grandchildren growing up has been a delight, raising them up in the way they should go, just as she did with her children.
